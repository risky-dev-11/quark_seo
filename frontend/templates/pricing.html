Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about our services and plans. If you need more help, feel free to contact our support team.

Do I need SEO knowledge to use aseotool?

No — aseotool is designed to be intuitive for both beginners and experts. Our clear instructions and explanations guide you through each step of the optimization process.

Which features are included in the Visitor (free) plan?

The Visitor plan lets anyone run a full SEO audit without creating an account. You get metadata & content checks, technical configuration and accessibility scans, plus a SERP snippet preview. No login required.

Which features are included in the Member (free) plan?

As a signed-in Member, you keep all Visitor features and also unlock progress tracking with charts, scheduled audits, and email notifications. Additionally, the Member plan provides performance metrics and highlights performance opportunities to help you further optimize your site — all at no cost.

Can I schedule automated reports and alerts?

Yes — Member and Premium users can set up daily, weekly, or monthly audits. You’ll receive concise email summaries of your site’s performance and any new issues, so you stay informed without having to log in.

How quickly will I see improvements in my ranking?

SEO improvements depend on many factors, including your website's current state and how quickly search engines re-crawl your site. Results may take several weeks to become noticeable.
